Pastries and Beverages

Beignet

A deep fried pastry made with choux pastry

Strawberry Scone

A quick bread made with leavened barley flour that is rolled into a round shape and cut into quarters

Soft, crumbly, pastries studded with fresh strawberries, jam, and cream, topped with a sweet glaze

Paris-brest

A dessert shaped like a bicycle wheel, studded with almonds

and dusted with powdered sugar

Pain au Chocolate

A type of viennoiserie pastry, "Pain au Chocolat" means chocolate bread and refers to a puff-pastry-like dessert

made with dough filled with pieces of dark chocolate or ganache

Macarons

One of the most visually striking and enticing pastries, it is a meringue-based cookie sandwich

It is light and delicate on the outside but buttery and smooth on the inside

Mocha

It is an espresso based drink, it is essentially a chocolate flavored type of cafe latte

It is made up of espresso, milk, and chocolate

Passion Fruit Tea

A refreshing beverage made using fresh pulp of passion fruit, tea leaves

and lime juice. It is bursting with tropical flavors and has a very unique tangy and sweet flavor
